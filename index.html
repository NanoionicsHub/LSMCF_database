This site includes both experimental and simulated data on La0.8Sr0.2(Mn, Co, Fe)O3¬±ùû≠ perovskite oxides with different Mn:Co:Fe ratios. The experimental data was measured with the following techniques: X-ray fluorescence and diffraction, Raman spectroscopy, ellipsometry, electrochemical impedance spectroscopy, and isotopic exchange depth profiling coupled to secondary ion mass spectrometry. All measurements were performed on a compositional map prepared by combinatorial pulsed laser deposition. The simulated data is predicted from the composition of Mn, Fe, and Co using a random forest model.
